.insight-list
  • Google Ads delivered the highest conversion volume (198) at £93 CPA, outperforming all other channels.
  • Meta Ads showed strong week-over-week improvement, with CPA dropping from £108 to £92 by month end.
  • LinkedIn Ads CPA of £85 is deceptively low — small sample size (21 conversions) makes this volatile.
  • TikTok Ads maintained consistent performance with minimal variance across weeks.
.exec-summary New

March 2026 delivered strong growth across paid channels. Total spend of £38,842 generated 412 conversions at a blended CPA of £94, exceeding the monthly target by 3%.

Google Ads remains the primary growth engine, accounting for 47% of all conversions. Meta Ads showed the strongest improvement trajectory, with CPA declining 15% over the course of the month. ROAS dipped slightly to 4.2x from 4.3x in February, driven by LinkedIn Ads testing costs.

.annotation New

Conversions are ML-modelled using SegmentStream's probabilistic attribution. CPA and ROAS values reflect attributed revenue, not last-click.

ROAS calculation uses 28-day attribution window. Figures may differ from platform-reported values due to cross-device matching.
